## Tips for Resume Parsing

1. **Contact Information**: Place email and phone at the top
2. **Clear Sections**: Use headers like SKILLS, EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION
3. **Location**: Include city and state/country
4. **Experience**: Mention years or duration clearly
5. **Skills**: List skills separated by commas
6. **Format**: Use standard fonts and avoid heavy formatting
7. **File Type**: Save as PDF for best results
